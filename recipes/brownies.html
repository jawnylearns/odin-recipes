Quick and Easy Brownies

Description

A chocolate brownie, or simply a brownie, is a chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Preperation

Step 1.

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x13-inch pan.

Step 2.

  1. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until combined.
  2. Fold in chopped walnuts.
  3. Spread the batter into the prepared pan.

Step 3.

  1. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. Cool before slicing into squares. Enjoy!
